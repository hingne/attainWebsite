About us

Welcome to Attain Infotech. We are a growing software solutions company committed to creating world class, innovative services and products that enable you to go beyond your current paradigms and create new realities.

Information Technology is revolutionizing the way we work by permeating in every area of our lives faster than we could ever imagine. Attain Infotech believes that the advantages of information technology should be available to individuals and enterprises free from the jargon and hype surrounding it.

Hence, standing in the customers' world, Attain Infotech creates easy-to-use services and products that directly cater to their immediate needs, solve their problems, supporting them create new opportunities for themselves.

Attain Infotech was established as a programming company. As time goes by, participating in the web development projects and facing the necessity of internet technologies to customer's business processes integration, Attain Infotech has founded its own Web Design Studio and Internet Marketing & Analytics department.

Our extensive experience and knowledge of emerging technology gives us the ability to provide a unique combination of strategy, design and technology for both Business-to-Business and Business-to-Consumer clients.

With our strong background in Internet consulting, graphic design, programming, multimedia and Internet marketing, we provide our clients with the business definitions and business strategy planning in the design and development of their web based solutions. We provide ongoing services that also include future site marketing, support and maintenance.
